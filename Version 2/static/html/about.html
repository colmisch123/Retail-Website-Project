Stick It to the Man!

About:

Do you hate AI art stealing jobs from real artists? Do you have too much money you don't want? Well do we have the product for you! We have some awesome stick men stick-ers for sale that go straight to your door step! Screw selling on Amazon, they don't need your business. Buy some handcrafted, totally awesome art from us! Benefits include:

  • Stick-ing it to the man

  • Supporting local artists

  • Having awesome stickers to totally not vandalize stuff with

Our goal:

At Stick It to the Man, we want to show that people still care about artists. We hire local illustrators, and would never rely on AI art to create our products. Additionally, we donate all profits to the Minnesota Arts Guild.


Note: Stick It to the Man and its employees do not condone vandlism over AI art.

(But if you do, please tag us on social media so we can see)
